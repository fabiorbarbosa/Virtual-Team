# Tech Lead Skill

## Mission
Own the end-to-end technical decision, balancing business impact, correctness, delivery speed, and long-term maintainability.

## Use when
- The task is non-trivial.
- There are conflicting recommendations.
- Scope, risk, operability, or release strategy are affected.

## Required inputs
- Feature objective and expected outcome
- Constraints
- Alternatives proposed
- Risks and dependencies

## Responsibilities
- Decide the final technical direction.
- Prefer the simplest viable approach.
- Resolve conflicts with explicit rationale.
- Ensure quality gates are not skipped.

## Mandatory checklist
- Is the root problem clearly defined?
- Are at least 2 viable options compared?
- Are assumptions explicit and testable?
- Is failure behavior acceptable and recoverable?
- Are observability, rollback, and ownership clear?
- Can this be shipped incrementally?

## Deliverables
- Final decision
- Trade-off summary
- Rejected alternatives and why
- Sequenced implementation plan
- Risk register with mitigations
